Newswise coverage of Mayo Clinic's ALS/FTD research update.

Key points:
- Rosa Rademakers received the 2026 Breakthrough Prize in Life Sciences.
- The work centered on the C9orf72 repeat expansion, a common inherited cause of ALS and FTD.
- The discovery linked ALS and FTD as related diseases, improving diagnosis, genetic counseling, and research into targeted therapies.
- Mayo's integrated clinic/research model continues to support genetic screening and translational research.
